Welcome to the About Us page of GEC Sreekrishnapuram Alumni Association, UAE Chapter!

At GEC Sreekrishnapuram, we believe that the bonds formed during our college years are lifelong treasures. The Alumni Association serves as a bridge, connecting past graduates and empowering them to build a strong network that spans across the globe. We are proud to present the UAE Chapter, a vibrant community of GEC Sreekrishnapuram alumni residing in the United Arab Emirates.

Our primary objective is to foster a sense of belonging and camaraderie among our alumni, irrespective of the distance that separates us. Through our dedicated efforts, we strive to bring together graduates from different batches, fostering an environment where memories are cherished, connections are reignited, and opportunities are created.

As the UAE Chapter, we organize a wide array of engaging events and initiatives designed to connect alumni, share experiences, and provide a platform for personal and professional growth. From networking events, knowledge-sharing sessions, and career development workshops to cultural gatherings, sports tournaments, and social outreach programs, we aim to create a diverse range of opportunities that cater to the varied interests and aspirations of our members.
